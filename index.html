About

The filmmaker
behind the lens

Every project starts long before the camera rolls. I invest heavily in pre-production, working through every creative and logistical detail so that once we are on set the execution is focused and deliberate. That same discipline carries through into post-production and the final edit. Underpinning all of it is a commitment to high-quality cinematography, because I believe the way something is shot is what gives a story its depth and resonance.

I'm a director and videographer based in Brisbane who has built experience across nearly every corner of the screen industry. Music video, documentary, commercial, real estate, live sports broadcast and narrative film. I've pursued that range deliberately because each format challenges and sharpens the craft in a different way.

I've operated solo, within small production crews, and across large broadcast teams, adapting quickly to whatever a project needs. A film school graduate from QUT, I bring structure and versatility to every project, always pushing my work in directing and cinematography further.
